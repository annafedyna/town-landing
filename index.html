Uzhhorod

ABOUT

The city of Uzhgorod is located on the foothills of the Carpathians, on the banks of the Uzh River. The city is the administrative, educational, scientific and cultural center of the Transcarpathian region. The territory occupies an area of ​​about 40 square meters. km, of which 1,574 ha are green massifs and plantations. The climate is moderately continental, with warm summers and mild winters.
